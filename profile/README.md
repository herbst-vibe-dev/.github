# Herbst Group

**Clarify. Empower. Elevate.**

Herbst Group is a pharmaceutical consulting firm specialising in Sales Force Effectiveness (SFE), commercial excellence, and data-driven decision intelligence for the healthcare industry.

Headquartered in Johannesburg, South Africa, we partner with pharmaceutical and healthcare companies across Africa, the Middle East, and Asia-Pacific to transform commercial operations through analytics, territory design, incentive architecture, and field force optimisation.

## What We Do

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **Sales Force Effectiveness** | Territory design, call planning, incentive structures, and performance analytics |
| **Commercial Intelligence** | Market surveillance, competitive analysis, and data platform engineering |
| **Regulatory & Medical Affairs** | Evidence dossiers, SAHPRA submissions, and pharmacovigilance support |
| **AI-Native Consulting** | Purpose-built AI infrastructure delivering consulting outputs at unprecedented speed and depth |

## Our Approach

We are an AI-native consulting firm. Every engagement is powered by proprietary infrastructure spanning 10 databases, 298 analytical tables, and 873 automated tests. This is not AI as a feature. It is the foundation of how we work.

Our **Herbst Conductor** methodology brings orchestral precision to sales force management: every representative is a conductor, every customer interaction is a performance, and every data point contributes to the score.
